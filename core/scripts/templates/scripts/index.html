welcome to the applescript tutorial

In the beginning there was the work of others. Smarter, more experinced people that came before and shared their knowledge. I started with simple scripts that filled needs missing in the production release of QLab. After reading a few and integrating them into my workflow the day came when I needed something new.

First I tried opening the editor and making something of my own. This was scary and I didn't really nderstand the moving pieces, and I failed miserably. The next time I needed something I decided to try and hack one of the scripts I already had. I changed a few variables, values, and properties, et voila - I had a new tool. This was the beginning of a bountiful relationship.

That was 5 years ago. Since then I've discovered a number of great resources, and built my own template file to improve my everyday workflow. This site serves as a walkthrough of that file, as well as a guide to learn how to hack together applescripts to fill the specific needs of your venue.
